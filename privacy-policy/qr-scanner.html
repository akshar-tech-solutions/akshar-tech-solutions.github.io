QR Scanner allows users to scan QR codes and barcodes, generate QR codes, save scan history, and perform actions such as opening websites, connecting to Wi-Fi networks, saving contacts, viewing locations, and adding calendar events.

All scan history and generated QR code records are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to share exported data.

The Application itself does not collect personal information directly. However, certain permissions and third-party SDKs may process limited data necessary for the Application to function.

  • Photos / Media Access — used when you choose to scan codes from images stored on your device.

The following third-party SDKs may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Google AdMob & Meta Audience Network — may collect advertising ID, IP address, device information, and usage signals for ad serving and personalisation in the free version.

  • Google Remote Config — may fetch configuration settings from Google servers. No personal data is transmitted.

No analytics, crash reporting, or behavioural tracking SDKs are intentionally used by the Application.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your personal information.
