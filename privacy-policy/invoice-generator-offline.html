This Privacy Policy applies to the Simple Invoice app (referred to as "Application"), developed by Akshar Tech (referred to as "Service Provider"). By using the Application, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. About This Application

Simple Invoice is an offline-first invoicing application. All invoice data, customer records, item catalogues, and business settings are stored exclusively on your device. The Application does not require an account, does not sync data to any cloud server, and does not transmit your business or invoice data to the Service Provider or any third party.

2. Information We Collect

The Application itself does not collect any personal information. The following data is not collected by the Application at any point:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Photos, files, or media from your device
  • Financial or payment information
  • Device contacts or call logs
  • Health or fitness data

However, the following third-party SDKs integrated into the Application may automatically collect limited technical data for the purposes described:

  • Google AdMob & Meta Audience Network — may collect device advertising ID, IP address, and device OS version for ad serving and personalisation. Active in the free version only.
  • Google Play Billing — handles in-app purchase transactions. No payment data is accessed or stored by the Application.
  • Package Info Plus — reads your app version and build number locally, only to display it in the About section and validate backup file compatibility. No data leaves your device.
  • Google Remote Config — may fetch remote configuration values from Google's servers. No personal data is transmitted.

No analytics, crash reporting, or behavioural tracking SDKs are used in this Application.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your personal data.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data you enter into the Application — including business profiles, customer records, invoice data, item catalogues, and settings — is stored locally on your device using a SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export a backup file using the built-in Backup & Restore feature.

When you use the Backup & Restore feature, you manually share your database file via your device's share sheet (e.g. to Google Drive, email, or local storage). The Service Provider has no access to this file.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates the following third-party services. These services operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Meta (Facebook) Audience Network
  • Google Remote Config

Users who upgrade to the Pro (ad-free) version will no longer be served ads, which reduces the data collected by advertising SDKs.
